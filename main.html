AnnaLee

AnnaLee is the founder of Her Campus Notre Dame. She founded the chapter in the winter of 2012 and at the time was the only writer for the chapter, through patience she gathered passionate people to begin to work for HCND. She is the foundation that the club is built upon and has created an amazingly successful online community.

Katie

Katie is the head editor at Her Campus Notre Dame. Katie makes sure that all of the women are creating pieces with journalistic integrity and without errors. She joined the HCND team originally as as a writer but her excellent editing skills shined through.

Lexi

Lexi deals with the management of Her Campus Notre Dame. She helps integrate new writers, send out daily reminders, manages the media, and helps keep the group organized. Originally just a writer, Lexi found her true passion in management. She will be returning next year in the same capacity.

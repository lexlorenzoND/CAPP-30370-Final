Her Campus is the #1 online community for college women. Written entirely by the nation’s top college journalists – with 3,000+ contributors and counting – HerCampus.com features national content on Style, Health, Love, Life, Politics and Career supplemented by local content from 200+ campus chapters across the country. In addition, Her Campus offers a daily email newsletter, social media communities, job listings, e-commerce, surveys, giveaways, and scholarships, filling a critical hole in the media marketplace and creating a robust interactive experience for its “collegiettes™” The Notre Dame chapter just launched last semester and is already garnering around 5,000 individual hits each week -- making it one of the most viewed chapters in the country. HCND provides an inclusive, fast-paced outlet for writing and publishing about college and campus life at ND and is continuously seeking passionate writers and contributors.
